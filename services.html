Services

Practical ways to bring P.A.T.H. into your recruiting team.

P.A.T.H. Recruiting Academy is built to help recruiting leaders and teams improve how recruiter conversations are structured, coached, and used to drive better hiring outcomes. These services are designed to move from theory into practical implementation.

Training Implementation Phone Screen Design Recruiting Playbooks

Core service offers

Clear ways to improve recruiter execution, consistency, and alignment.

These are the most direct ways P.A.T.H. can support your organization. Some teams need training. Others need structure. Others need help redesigning how recruiter conversations are happening today.

Service 1

Recruiter Training Workshops

Train recruiters to lead more consultative, structured, and effective candidate conversations that improve discovery, alignment, and next-step quality.

  • P.A.T.H. conversation model overview
  • Question-driven recruiting approach
  • Candidate discovery and motivation work
  • Live examples and practical application
Service 2

Framework Implementation Support

Put a repeatable conversation structure in place that recruiters can actually use and leaders can coach across the team.

  • Framework customization by team need
  • Recruiter usage guidance
  • Manager and leader coaching support
  • Practical roll-out structure
Service 3

Phone Screen Design

Redesign early-stage recruiter conversations so qualification, candidate engagement, and handoff quality improve before interviews ever begin.

  • Phone screen structure
  • Candidate discovery flow
  • Transition language and alignment points
  • Role-specific question design
Service 4

Hiring Manager Alignment Tools

Improve how recruiting and hiring leaders align on role expectations, candidate signals, and what quality actually looks like in the process.

  • Intake meeting structure
  • Alignment questions
  • Success profile clarification
  • Interview handoff support
Service 5

Recruiting Playbooks and Toolkits

Give your team usable materials that turn the framework into daily execution instead of something that only lives in training.

  • Conversation guides
  • Question libraries
  • Phone screen templates
  • Objection handling frameworks
Service 6

Advisory and Consulting Support

Work through broader recruiting process issues where conversation quality, recruiter execution, and process alignment are affecting hiring performance.

  • Team-level recruiting review
  • Execution gap analysis
  • Process quality support
  • Strategy discussion and guidance

Best fit

Who these services are designed for.

P.A.T.H. is a strong fit for organizations that care about improving the quality of the recruiting conversation, not just increasing activity volume.

Internal Recruiting Teams

Teams that want more structure, better phone screens, stronger discovery, and more consistency across recruiters.

Talent Acquisition Leaders

Leaders who want a framework they can coach, reinforce, and use to improve recruiting execution.

Growing Organizations

Companies that need a better process as hiring pressure rises and the cost of weak conversations becomes more visible.
