What makes the approach different

A more structured way to lead the recruiting conversation.

P.A.T.H. is built around question-driven candidate discovery. Instead of relying on job pitching, recruiters are trained to guide conversations that uncover motivation, clarify fit, and create stronger alignment between candidate goals and company opportunity.

Candidate Discovery
